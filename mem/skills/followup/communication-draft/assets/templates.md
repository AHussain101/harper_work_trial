# Communication Templates

## Email Templates

### New Lead Follow-Up

```
Subject: Quick question about your insurance needs - {Company}

Hi {Name},

Thanks for reaching out about insurance for {Company}. I'd love to learn more about your business and help find the right coverage.

Do you have 15 minutes this week for a quick call? I have availability [suggest 2-3 times].

Best,
{Agent Name}
Harper Insurance
```

### Application Follow-Up

### Quote Follow-Up

```
Subject: Checking in on your {Insurance Type} quote

Hi {Name},

I wanted to follow up on the quote we discussed for {Company}. Have you had a chance to review the options?

I'm happy to walk through the coverage details again or answer any questions. We can also explore other carrier options if the pricing doesn't work.

What works best for you?

Best,
{Agent Name}
```

## Call Script Templates

### Quote Follow-Up Call

**Opening:**
"Hi {Name}, this is {Agent} from Harper. I'm calling to follow up on the {Insurance Type} quote we sent over for {Company}."

**Key Questions:**
- "Have you had a chance to review the quote?"
- "Any questions about the coverage or pricing?"
- "Is there anything holding you back from moving forward?"

**Objection Handling:**
- Price too high → "I can check with other carriers for alternatives"
- Need more time → "When would be a good time to reconnect?"
- Need to discuss internally → "Happy to join a call with your team"

**Close:**
"What would you need from me to feel comfortable moving forward?"
